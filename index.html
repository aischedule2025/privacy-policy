Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 31 May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mobile World (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles personal information through our internal staff mobile application (the “AI Scheduling App”).

1. Purpose of the App

The AI Scheduling App is designed exclusively for staff members of Mobile World to manage their personal working schedules and preferences. It supports automatic staff assignment based on each staff member's:

  • Preferred working days
  • Actual assigned days
  • Teaching subjects and levels

2. What Data the App Uses

The AI Scheduling App accesses and displays the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Teaching subjects and levels
  • Assigned working days
  • Preferred working days
  • Profile picture
  • History/Status of leave requests

This data is collected and stored via our administrative website, and is not collected directly by the AI Scheduling App, except where noted below.

3. Data Collected Within the App

The AI Scheduling App only allows staff to:

  • Upload or change their profile picture
  • Update their preferred working days
  • Submit leave requests
  • Approve or decline assignment as a temporary staff from another staff's leave request

These updates are sent securely to our backend database, which is shared with the administrative web platform.

4. Data Access and Visibility

Each staff member has their own login and can only view their own profile and working schedule. No staff member can access other users’ data through the AI Scheduling App

5. How We Use Your Data

The data accessed or updated in the AI Scheduling App is used solely to:

  • Display your schedule and assignments
  • Automatically generate staff assignments
  • Support internal workforce planning

We do not use this data for advertising or marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

All communication between the AI Scheduling App and our server is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS), which ensures your data is securely transmitted over the internet.

7. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with any third-party services, advertisers, or external entities. It is used only within Mobile World for internal operational purposes.

8. Data Retention

Your information is stored securely and retained as long as you remain an active staff member.

9. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and view your personal information within the AI Scheduling App
  • Update your preferred working days and profile picture
  • Contact us to request correction or deletion of your information
